Tea-Creations

  1. boil water in a kettle or pot
  2. place a tea bagnor loose tea in a cup or teapot
  3. pour the boiling water over the tea
  4. let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes
  5. remove the tea bag or strain the loose tea
  6. add sweetener,milk,or lemon as desired
  7. stir and enjoy your cup of tea
